Invoice Billing Policy

Welcome to StrategiRise LLC. This Invoice Billing Policy outlines our procedures for issuing and managing invoices, ensuring transparency and efficiency in our billing practices.

1. Invoice Issuance

Invoices are issued for all services provided by StrategiRise LLC. Upon completion of a service or upon reaching a milestone in a service agreement, an invoice will be generated and sent to the client. Invoices will include the following details:

  • Invoice number
  • Date of issue
  • Description of services provided
  • Total amount due
  • Payment terms and due date

Invoices will be sent electronically to the email address provided by the client. If you require a physical copy, please request it during the service agreement process.

2. Payment Terms

Payments are due within 30 days from the date of the invoice unless otherwise specified in the service agreement. Late payments may incur additional charges as detailed below:

  • Late Fees: A late fee of 1.5% per month will be applied to overdue amounts.
  • Interest Charges: Interest charges will accrue on overdue amounts at a rate of 18% per annum.

3. Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer
  • Credit/Debit Card
  • Online Payment Platforms (e.g., PayPal)

Payment details and instructions will be included on the invoice. Please ensure that payments are made in accordance with the instructions provided to avoid processing delays.

4. Dispute Resolution

If you have any disputes or concerns regarding an invoice, please contact us within 15 days of receiving the invoice. Disputes should be addressed in writing and sent to:

  • Email: contact@strategirise.com

We will review and respond to your dispute promptly. Any agreed-upon adjustments or corrections will be reflected in a revised invoice.

5. Refunds and Adjustments

Refunds or adjustments to invoices may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Billing Errors: If an error is found in the invoice amount or service description.
  • Service Adjustments: If services were not delivered as agreed upon and corrections are required.

Requests for refunds or adjustments should be submitted within 30 days of receiving the invoice. We will review and address these requests in a timely manner.

6. Changes to This Billing Policy

StrategiRise LLC may update this Invoice Billing Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our billing practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the beginning of this policy will indicate the most recent revisions. Clients are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
